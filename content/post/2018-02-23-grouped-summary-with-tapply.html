COPY / PASTE HERE:

tapply( d.in$metric, d.in$group, summary)

This is a quick one and sourced from this SO answer but I kept googling it so wanted to just put it up here.

I’ll often want to quickly view 5-stat summaries of a grouped dataframe. tapply isn’t really a purrr or dplyr approach so people might not think it is elegant but everything I’m googling for purrr or dplyr solutions to this problem statement are really verbose. This is a one-liner.

tapply(mtcars$wt, mtcars$cyl, summary)
